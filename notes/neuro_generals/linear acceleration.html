Linear acceleration

Part of

vestibular system

Definition

linear acceleration = when a lift moves, or your body leans to one side

Located in

detected by utricle (horizontal) and saccule (vertical)

Details

macula = sheet of hair cells

otolith = clump of small crystals

otolith and hair cells are embedded in a gelatinous mass

when you move to one side, the inertia of the otolith drags on the hair cells
