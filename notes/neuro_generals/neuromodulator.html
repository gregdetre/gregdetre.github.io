from fn2 1270

"These so-called neuromodulatory systems originate in the brainstem and send widespread projections into cortical and subcortical regions. Unlike neurons using classical neurotransmitters such as GABA or glutamate, neurons that use neuromodulators do not directly excite or inhibit their postsynaptic targets. Instead, they modulate the responess of target neurons to classical neuromodulator inputs."
