Voice onset time

Aka

VOT

Definition

length of time between movement of the vocal folds and air pressure being produced against them by the voice box

lag time between the opening of the lips and the opening/closing of the vocal cords

Experiments

the phonemes /ba/ and /pa/ are differentiated by VOT

/ba/ VOT = 30ms (0-20ms) voiced consonant

/pa/ VOT = 60ms (40-120ms) voiceless consonant

both labial (articulated by the lips)

speech perception is partly the business of categorising continua, like VOT, in order to distinguish different phonemes in order to recognise words
