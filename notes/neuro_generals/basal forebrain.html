Basal forebrain

info from http://www.memorylossonline.com/glossary/basalforebrain.html

subcortical

near the bottom of the front of the brain

Consists of

nucleus bsalis

diagonal band

medial septum

substantia innominata

Function

produce acetylcholine

damaging the basal forebrain -> reduced acetylcholine

impaired learning and memory (amnesia, confabulation)

often caused by aneurysm of the anterior communicating artery.
