Somatosensory fibers

Located in

dorsal root

Details

somatosensory fibers - classified by diameter/conduction velocity

Aa fibers

some of the proprioceptive neurons, e.g. stretch receptors

Ab fibers

all of the discriminative touch receptors

Ad fibers

fast pain

instantaneous, e.g. when your arm jerks back before you've even noticed the pain

sharp, piercing

rapidly adapting

C fibers

slow pain

small, unmyelinated, slow

mediated by the tissue-damage peptides

slow adapting

distress, dull ache, does not trigger withdrawl reflexes
