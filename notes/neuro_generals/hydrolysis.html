Hydrolysis

hydrolysis (wik) = chemical reaction or process in which a molecule is split into two parts by reacting with a molecule of water

one of the parts gets an OH- from the water molecule and the other part gets an H+ from the water.

ATP hydrolysis =

ATP + H2O <=> ADP + P_i
