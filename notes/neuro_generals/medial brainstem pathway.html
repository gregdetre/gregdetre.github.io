Medial brainstem pathway

Aka

medial descending system

Located in

one of the two motor pathways from the brainstem nuclei to the spinal cord - see also lateral brainstem pathway

somehow may get collaterals from the ventral corticospinal tract

Consists of

three tracts in the medial pathway:

  • vestibulospinal tract
  • tectospinal tract
  • reticulospinal tract

Function

basic postural control system

descend ipsilaterally down ventral columns of the spinal cord

terminate on interneurons and long propriospinal neurons

affect axial muscles in ventromedial portion of spinal cord
