Transcortical motor aphasia

Part of

aphasia

Located in

damage to

dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (ongoing control of speech)

possibly Brocas area

left supplementary motor area (initiation of speech)

Definition

like Brocas aphasia, but repetition is spared

Unimpaired

comprehension

repetition - even very long sentences

Impaired

nonfluent, explosive production

sometimes right-sided weakness

Mechanism

Geschwind-Wernicke model explains this as preserved connection to Wernickes area

see perisylvian area
