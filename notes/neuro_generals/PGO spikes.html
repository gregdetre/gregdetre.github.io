PGO spikes

areas that fire in more intense bursts during REM sleep (correlate with bursts of eye movements):

pons

lateral geniculate nucleus

occipital cortex

generate ponto-geniculo-occipital (PGO) spikes

originate in pontine reticular formtaion

propagate through the lateral geniculate nucleus to the occipital cortex

resemble the startle response in awake subjects
