Medulla

Aka

medulla oblongata

Located in

brainstem

Consists of

medullary pyramids

olivary bodies

A1/A2 norepinephrine cell groups

sends to hypothalamus

Connects

links the spinal cord and the brain

Function

controls involuntary muscular and glandular activities

breathing, heart contraction, artery dilation, salivation, vomiting, probably laughing
