Cerebellar cortex

only 5 types of neurons

  • stellate cells (inhibitory)
  • basket cells (inhibitory)
  • Purkinje cells (inhibitory)
  • Golgi cells (inhibitory)
  • granule cells (excitatory)

3 layers (from outer to inner):

  • molecular layer
  • Purkinje layer
  • granular layer

Function

modulate information flowing through the deep cerebellar nuclei
