Betz cells

Definition

giant pyramidal cells in layer V (internal pyramidal layer) of primary motor cortex

Sends to

corticospinal tract -> spinal cord

also

corticomesencephalic tract

long axons to the motor cranial nerve nuclei

mainly in contralateral side of the midbrain

corticopontine tract -> pons

corticobulbar tract -> medulla

Mechanism

pyramidal decussation tract = where many of the corticospinal tract fibers cross over to the contralateral side in the medulla

85% cross and become the lateral corticospinal tract

15% don't cross and become the medial corticospinal tract
