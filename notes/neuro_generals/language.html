Grammar

grammar =

system that specifies how vocabulary units can be combined into words, phrases and sentences

and how the meanings of a combination can be determined by the meanings onf the units and the way they're arranged

3 components to grammar

  • morphology
  • syntax
  • phonology

Morphology

morphology = rules for combining words and affixes into larger words, e.g. bite + s

Syntax

syntax = rules for combining words into phrases and sentences and determining relationships among words

  • grouping sequences of words into phrases
  • verb specifies the semantic roles of its arguments
  • two phrases in a sentence can be linked to refer to the same entity in the world

    cf anaphora, deep vs surface structure

Phonology

phonology = rules for combining sounds into a consistent pattern

incl prosody (intonation, stress, timing over phrases/snetences)
