Blobs

Definition

blobs = dark, peg-like regions about 0.2mm in diameter

as revealed by staining for mitochondrial enzyme cytochrome oxidase

Located in

superficial V1 layers 2 and 3

with intervening lighter interblobs

Inputs from

especially koniocellular

and parvocellular

also some magnocellular (ks4 551)

Send to

thin stripes in V2 (thence to V4)

extensive local connections between V1 blobs and interblobs

Mechanism

colour-opponent cells, not very orientation sensitive

blobs receive inputs from parvocellular, magnocellular and koniocellular streams

to V2 thin stripes to V4

V2 thin stripes are colour selective

blob system

blobs of V1 layers 2+3 -> V2 thin stripes -> V4 -> inferior temporal cortex
