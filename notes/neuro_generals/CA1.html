Function

having minimized overlap in CA3, you then need to link the non-overlapping representations you've encoded back to highly overlapping EC patterns

this reverse mapping is highly prone to interference

it's like a translator, with an intermediate representation (halfway between the sparseness of CA3 than entorhinal cortex), that really helps
