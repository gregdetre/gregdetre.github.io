Oculomotor nucleus

Aka

Edinger-Westphal nucleus

parvocellular oculomotor nucleus

Part of

extraocular motor neurons

Located in

rostral midbrain at the level of the superior colliculus

Sends to

medial, inferior and superior recti and inferior oblique extraocular muscles via the oculomotor nerve

Function

involved in accommodation and vergence

and the constricting muscles of the iris

broken up into lots of smaller nuclei, each controlling a different one of the extraocular muscles
