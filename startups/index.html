Memrise (2008-2012; 2022), co-founder; CTO; cameo

Before that, I co-founded Memrise.com with Grandmaster of Memory Ed Cooke in 2008. Memrise blends the art and science of memory to power incredibly effective, social and playful online learning experiences. We have more than 4m users around the world, who have produced amazing and memorable courses for more than 100 languages, as well as everything from maps to morse code to mushrooms.

Give Memrise a try!

I worked as the CEO from 2008-2011, the CTO from 2008-2012. Though I'm now retired from active Memrise service, we're all still friends, I own shares and remain a huge cheerleader :)
