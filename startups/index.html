Memrise (2008-2012; 2022), co-founder; CTO; cameo

I co-founded the Memrise app for language learning (with Ed Cooke and Ben Whately), now a global brand with 70m users, awarded overall Best App in the 2017 Google Play Awards and TIMEâ€™s #2 EdTech company of 2024. See also: TIME 2024 World EdTech #2 company in the world; Guardian (also); Independent; TechCrunch; MIT Technology Review.

I worked as the CEO from 2008-2011, the CTO from 2008-2012, and came back for a cameo to work on the AI-powered MemBot in 2022.

We blended the art and science of memory to produce a really effective, evidence-based general-purpose learning platform. Our global community produced amazing and memorable courses for more than 100 languages, as well as everything from maps to morse code to mushrooms. Memrise now focuses on language-learning, helping you 1) learn the vocabulary, 2) immerse yourself in how the language sounds, and 3) practice conversation with AI.

Big Health/Sleepio (2014-2016), CTO

I was the CTO at Big Health, which develops best-in-class evidence-based mental health apps.

I helped scale up their astonishingly-effective Sleepio product, bringing together a great team, building the app, getting everything HIPAA-ready, all while the company moved from London to the US. Big Health has since gone from strength to strength - the NHS makes wide use of their apps, and the FDA treats them as the first 'non-drug treatments'.
