Muscle disorders

Part of

motor disorders

Examples

atrophy = decrease in muscle mass

sarcopenia = gradual decrease in skeletal muscle function and mass with aging

may result from

gradual failure in satellite cells (help regenerate skeletal muscle fibers)

less of growth factors

dystrophy = structural defects in the muscle

myopathy = inflammatory reactions in the body directed against muscle

Tests

test creatine kinase levels in the blood

electromyography - measuring electrical activity in muscles

muscle biopsy - identify a myopathy

genetic testing
