Reuptake

Definition

reuptake = neurotransmitter from the cleft is broken down or taken back into the axon terminal

Details

you don't want your neurotransmitter just floating around, binding and rebinding

mechanisms for taking apart the neurotransmitter, and reuptake mechanisms in the presynaptic cell

theory that serotonin and other monoamines are related to depression - when you have too much of them

so in order to have more serotonin hanging around the synapses, you can just block the reuptake mechanism
