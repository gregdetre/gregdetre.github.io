Aka

amnesic-confabulatory syndrome

severe retrograde amnesia and anterograde amnesia

confabulation

Mechanism

caused by damage to

mammillary bodies and other regions (dorsomedial nucleus of the thalamus)

from deficiency of thiamine (vitamin B1)

or chronic alcholism
