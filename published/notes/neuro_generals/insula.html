Insula

Aka

island of Reil

Input from

thalamus

Located in

island of cortex buried deep inside the cerebral hemispheres

within the lateral sulcus separating the temporal lobe and inferior parietal cortex

Function

important for planning or coordinating articulation

see language#other_brain_areas

Mechanism

lesions -> difficulty pronouncing phonomes in proper order

usually produce combinations of sounds close to the target word

no dififculty perceiving speech sounds or recognizing their own errors

no trouble finding the word, only producing it

also damaged in patients with Brocas aphasia

Etymology

insula = Latin for 'island'
