Spatial attention

Part of

attention

Consists of

hemispatial neglect

visual extinction

Mechanism

spatial cuing, cf Posner paradigm

fronto-cingulo-parietal network for control of spatial attention (Mesulam, 1999)

other areas

increased baseline activity during expectation period

increased even more after stimulus appeared

  • frontal eye fields
  • supplementary eye fields
  • anterior cingulate cortex

Bushnell et al (1981)

monkeys fixated while peripheral stimuli were flashed

showed posterior parietal cortex activity in receptive fields for peripheral stimuli, even though no overt response

even stronger responses when the monkeys had to respond to dimming of the peripheral stimuli (i.e. they were now behaviorally significant)

directing attention -> increased contralateral hemifield activity in extrastiate cortex (Heinze et al, 1994)

Clinical

dorsal simultanagnosia

ventral simultanagnosia
