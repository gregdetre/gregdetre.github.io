Membrane potential

Definition

membrane potential (electric voltage) at neuron cell body

membrane potential = voltage = difference in electrical charge of the neuron relative to extracellular space

Details

two mechanisms preserve an imbalance in ion concentrations across the cell membrane

sodium-potassium pump

selective permeability of channels

a given channel will only allow a couple of types of ions to pass

many of these channels are closed unless specifically activated, i.e. open and close as a function of the inputs coming into the neuron
