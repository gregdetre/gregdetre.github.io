Definition

rapid involuntary rhythmic eye movement

eye move quickly in one direction (quick phase) and then slowly in the other direction

both physiological and pathological kinds of nystagmus

direction of nystagmus defined by the direction of its quick phase

semicircular nystagmus movement

= downbeat, upbeat, seesaw, periodic alternating or pendular nysagmus

Function

"combination of a slow adjusting eye movement (slow phase) like would be seen with the vestibulo-ocular reflex, followed by a quick saccade (quick phase) when the eye has reached the limit of its rotation"

(from wikipedia)

Related to

saccadic oscillations (opsoclonus or ocular flutter)

Mechanism

apparently a symptom of Wernickes encephalopathy
