Anatomical directions

in human head

superior = dorsal = up

inferior = ventral = down

anterior = rostral = front

posterior = caudal = rear

in human spine = as in quadruped animals

in quadruped animals

ventral = towards their stomach, down

dorsal = towards their back, up

rostral = towards the end of the nose

caudal = towards the tail

medial = towards the middle

lateral = out from the middle, to the sides
