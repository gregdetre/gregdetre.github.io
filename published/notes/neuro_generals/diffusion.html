Diffusion

diffusion = natural tendency of particles is to spread out in a liquid or gas

causes particulars of a given type to be evenly distributed throughout space

acts on concentration differences (rather than charge differences)

diffusion current - reduces concentration potential/gradient

diffusion coefficient = like the electrical conductance

I = -DC (Fick's First Lw)

where

I = movement of ions (diffusion current)

D = diffusion coefficient

C = concentration potential
