Muscle groups

to make a movement - involves contraction of muscle groups

activates agonist muscle a little too vigorously

then activates antagonist muscle with slight delay

to get rid of the the excess agonist torque

sometimes also a third phase to stop the agonist muscle exactly on target

muscles can only pull, not push

so hinge joints require at least two muscles pulling in opposite directions

= antagonist muscles

program muscle groups together

since even though muscles act directly on the joints they cross

they affect multiple remote joints
