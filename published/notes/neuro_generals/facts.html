Audition

hair cells

sound vibrates tympanic membrane

stapes (bone inside tympanic membrane) pushes against oval window

displaces fluid in cochlea, causes deflection of basilar membrane

inner hair cells in basilar membrane get pushed by tectorial membrane above them, deflecting them

inside the hair cells, the tip links connecting the stereocilia to the kinicilium stretch, opening the channels

lets in the K+, depolarizes, opens voltage-gated Ca++ channels
