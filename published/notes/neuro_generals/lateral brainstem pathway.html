Lateral brainstem pathway

Aka

rubrospinal tract

lateral descending system

Located in

one of the two motor pathways from the brainstem nuclei to the spinal cord - see also medial brainstem pathway

Input from

red nucleus (brainstem nuclei)

Function

more concerned with goal-directed limbic movements such as reaching and manipulating/grasping

crosses contralaterally

terminate on interneurons in the dorsolateral part of the spinal cord gray matter

influence distal muscles
