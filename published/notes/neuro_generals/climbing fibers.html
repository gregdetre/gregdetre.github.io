Climbing fibers

Part of

premotor-cerebello-rubrocerebellar loop

Located in

cerebellum

Input from

inferior olivary nucleus

Sends to

powerfully excites 1-10 Purkinje cells -> complex spike

Mechanism

carry somatosensory, visual or information from cerebral cortex

very specific

in contrast to massive convergence and divergence of cerebellar mossy fibers and parallel fibers

v low rate of spontaneous firing

only partially affected by sensory stimuli

if lesioned, produces a large increase in frequency of simple spikes

can induce selective LTD in parallel fibers that are active concurrently

Etymology

wrap around the cell bodies and proximal dendrites of Purkinje cells like a vine on a tree

mnemonic: 'climb the olive tree'
