Vestibular system

information on movements of the head and the position of the head relative to gravity

angular acceleration = detecting rotation

semi-circular canals - fluid filled hoops - tell you which way the fluid is flowing in the canals

linear acceleration = when a lift moves, or your body leans to one side

very sensitive to toxins in the blood
