Biography

After studying at Oxford and Harvard, Greg received a PhD in computational neuroscience from Princeton, reading people's minds with machine learning and brain scanners to understand why we forget things. He co-founded Memrise, now TIME #2 EdTech company in the world with 70m users, and while Chief Data Scientist his Channel 4 team won the DataIQ Best data and analytics team of 2019. He now helps AI and software development teams as an advisor and coach.
