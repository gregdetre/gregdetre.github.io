Biography

After studying at Oxford and Harvard, Greg received a PhD in computational neuroscience from Princeton, reading people's minds with machine learning and brain scanners to understand why we forget things. He co-founded Memrise, the TIME #2 EdTech company in the world with 70m users, and won the DataIQ Best data and analytics team of 2019 as Chief Data Scientist at Channel 4. Along the way, he led technology, product, and AI/data science teams at The Guardian, Big Health/Sleepio, Filtered, and others. He now helps companies build great software products and use AI in imaginative ways.
