Healthyself Journal

Healthyself is a voice-first, privacy-first journaling app with warm, supportive, evidence-based questions to deepen your self-reflection.

  • Key features
  • Choose your AI
  • Asks warm, supportive, evidence-based questions

Key features

  • Voice recording - Express yourself naturally through speech instead of typing.

  • Warm, supportive, evidence-based questions. Drawing on dozens of research papers and techniques, including gratitude, redemptive narrative construction, mindfulness, famous coaching questions, and many more.

  • Privacy-first. Your data is yours. We don't have any access to it, and we prioritise security and resilience.

  • Choose your AI. Either on-device or best-in-class cloud (with your API keys).

  • Dropbox sync (optional).

  • Open source.

Choose your AI

For local AI, you'll have to choose & download the models.

For cloud transcription and AI features, you'll need to provide your own API keys:

  • OpenAI API key for transcription (required) and speech output (optional)

  • Anthropic API key for AI questions (required)

(Enter these once in Settings and they're stored securely on your device. We never see your keys.)

Asks warm, supportive, evidence-based questions

Drawing on 30+ research papers, including:

  • Self-distancing techniques

  • Structured reflection vs rumination

  • Cognitive-emotional integration

  • Implementation intentions

  • Optimal session timing

  • Gratitude practice

  • Redemptive narrative construction

  • Progress tracking

  • Socratic questioning

  • Mindfulness and body scans

  • Explanatory style and Seligman's Three P's

And

  • A bank of exploratory questions

  • Contemplative practices, such as the Beginning Anew practice (Plum Village tradition)

  • Famous coaching frameworks
