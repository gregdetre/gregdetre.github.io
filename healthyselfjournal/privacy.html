Healthyself Journal - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 September 2025

The Short Version

Healthyself is a private journal. We can't read your journal entries because we never receive them. Your data stays on your phone, unless you enable cloud AI services or syncing (e.g. with Dropbox).

What We Collect

Nothing.

We don't collect:

  • Your journal entries

  • Your voice recordings

  • Your personal information

  • Analytics or usage data

  • Your API keys

Where Your Data Goes

Your journal stays on your device:

  • Voice recordings and transcripts: Stored locally on your phone

  • API keys (if you provide them): Stored securely on your phone

Optional services you control:

  • Your API keys: When you use OpenAI or Anthropic, your data goes directly from your phone to their services. We never see it.

  • Dropbox sync: If you connect Dropbox, your journal syncs to YOUR Dropbox account, not ours.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data. However, since we don't collect any personal data, these rights are automatically fulfilled:

  • Right to access: You already have all your data on your device

  • Right to deletion: Delete the app (and optional Dropbox folder in Dropbox/Apps/) to remove all local data

  • Right to portability: Your data is already portable via Dropbox export

  • Right to correction: Edit your entries directly in the app

Children

Healthyself is rated 12+ due to potential medical/mental health content in the questions. We don't knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Third-Party Services

If you choose to use them:

  • OpenAI: Read their privacy policy at openai.com/privacy

  • Anthropic: Read their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy

  • Dropbox: Read their privacy policy at dropbox.com/privacy

These are optional. You connect directly using your own accounts.

Security

  • Your journal is protected by your device's security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode)

  • API keys are stored in iOS Keychain (secure storage)

  • We use HTTPS for all network connections

  • The app is open source for transparency

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top. Since we don't have your contact details, check back occasionally if you're interested.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email: privacy@healthyselfjournal.org

Legal Basis

We operate under the principle of "privacy by design" - we've built the app so that we cannot access your data. This complies with UK GDPR requirements by not processing personal data at all.

Data Controller: Not applicable (no data collection)

Data Protection Registration: Not required (no data processing)

Jurisdiction: United Kingdom
