Matlab cookbook

I'm a big fan of O'Reilly's Python Cookbook. Since O'Reilly hasn't published one for Matlab, and it's the language I know best, I'd dreamed of writing one myself.

That's a gargantuan task. So, in the meantime, I've had a go at collecting and posting a few tips and scripts that I thought others might find useful. To the Matlab Cookbook!
